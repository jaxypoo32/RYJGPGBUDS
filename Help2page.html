How to use the Google Helper

To use the google helper, first you need to sign in to your google account. Once you are signed in, you can access the helper by clicking on the helper icon located on the top right corner of the webpage. The helper will provide you with various tools and resources to assist you with your tasks. You can use the helper to set reminders, create to-do lists, and access important documents. Make sure to explore all the features of the helper to get the most out of it!
